Save It Transform your savings into success.

UX/UI Case Study

Project Scope

  • Brief:To design a money saving app that motivates its users to save money through a personalized experience, allowing them to set goals and achieve milestones while having an overview of their spending.
  • Problem:Saving can be difficult, intimidating and overwhelming. Many people struggle to start and can be pushed even further away by complex financial jargon.
  • Solution:An application made specifically for those who struggle to save, including personalized advice, simple interface and encouraging copy.
  • Tools Used:Figma, Balsamiq, AI (Board of Innovation)

  • Process:User research, user journeys, wireframing, usability testing, branding, design system, prototyping.

What motivates people to save money? How can technology encourage a positive outlook on financing?

Step 1: Interviews

With the help of AI, I created an interview script aimed at understanding what motivates people to save money, and what they look for in their money saving applications.

  • I want to be able to have an overview of my accounts and savings in one interface.
  • I want to be able to lock my savings so I don't overspend.
  • I want an overview of my monthly spending and advice on how I can save more.

Step 2: User Flow

After understanding user needs, I began mapping the user journey. With three main flows, I made sure that each path was simple, easy to understand and motivational.

Starting with pen & paper...

Step 4: Mid Fidelity Wireframes

A feedback driven cycle.

Step 5: User Testing

Using a prototype made with mid-fidelity wireframes, I conducted a usability test with three potential users. I asked them to create a new goal, edit a goal, add a new account. Additionally, I conducted a preference test regarding the monthly report, with a unanimous vote on the personalized reporting style.

User 1

  • Home Page:It looks great, very clear, but I wish there was a way to add an account directly from the homepage.
  • Goals:I can easily find the option to add a new goal, but I wish the button was a bit bigger and more obvious, considering its a key feature. I would also like to be able to find the edit option easily, rather than opening a new menu.
  • Profile:I think making it obvious which text is clickable is important, but everything is clear.
  • Report:This feature is great and very useful, I use something similar regularly.

User 2

  • Home Page:Everything is clear and looks good, but I think you should be able to add or edit your accounts from the homepage.
  • Goals:I can find the new goal option easily, but I think the button could be clearer with some text. The process is intuitive and easy to understand.
  • Profile:The navigation process is easy to follow and very simple.
  • Report:I believe this feature is very useful and is suitable to this type of application.

User 3

  • Home Page:Everything is easy to understand and is very clear.
  • Goals:I like that you have the option to deposit the amount automatically every month with a new goal. I would add a more motivating confirmation message when adding a new goal.
  • Profile:Everything is easy to navigate and makes sense.
  • Report:This is a great and very suitable feature for the application, very useful.

Key Findings

  1. Ensure users are able to manage/add accounts from homepage.

  2. Make “add new goal” button more obvious.

  3. Make text more encouraging.

  4. Ensure strong text hierarchy throughout all screens.

  5. Make it easier to deposit money into a goal.

A brand that considers its users first.

Brand Guidelines

  • Trustworthy Save it understands how difficult saving money can be, and that’s why it builds a relationship with its user, gaining their trust, encouraging them every step of the way, until they reach their goals.
  • Simple Not everyone is tech-crazy, that’s why Save it’s interface can be understood by just about anyone. Simplicity is key, and Save it provides just that.
  • Friendly Finance is intimidating, and many steer away from it, feeling like it isn’t their place. Save it lets people know that anyone can save, and it will be there guiding you regardless of your goal.

Logo

A simple typeface logo with an upwards arrow at the end, letting users know that with this application, you can only get closer to your goals. The logo can be used in a dark blue or white, depending on the background.

The logo should not:

Be stretched or compressed.

Be used in a non-brand colour.

Colour

Navy blue is the primary colour and will be used for background. A dark mode application will not only be easier to read, but will also give off a sense of professionalism.

The remaining three colours will be used as secondary colours throughout the application. They will be used to distinguish between types of savings and actions, this is why shades of each colour have been incorporated, giving room for a wide range of colours.

All colours have been tested for contrast accessibility and pass the WCAG AAA standard. This was done considering the dark blue as the background colour, and the remaining three as foreground colours.

White is to be used for text.

Typography

Text should be easy to read Outfit, 40, Bold

Just as saving is meant to be Outfit, 32, Semi-bold

Which is why a sans-serif font works best Outfit, 24, Medium

With a friendly, simple, and spacious feel Outfit, 20, Regular

Illustrations

To be used sparingly so as not to overwhelm the app, and to provide encouragement and joy during celebratory moments.

Writing Style

  • Concise:Users should be able to use the application on the go, and quickly understand the information in front of them, which is why writing should be easy to understand and concise.

  • Simple:No difficult jargon should be used. All statements must be straight to the points and understood by everyone regardless of educational background.

  • Encouraging:The application should ensure that users reach their saving goals, text must be uplifting and encouraging, reminding users of how far they’ve gotten and congratulating them when reaching their milestones.
